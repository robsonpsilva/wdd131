Pedra Bonita Trail

Location:The trail begins on Estrada da Pedra Bonita, between Pedra da Gávea and the neighborhoods of São Conrado and Barra da Tijuca in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Description:The Pedra Bonita trail, in the Tijuca National Park, is a route of 1,257 meters one way, with an altitude of 693 meters

Iguaçu Falls Trail

Location:The waterfalls trail is accessible through the Cataratas National Park located in Foz do Iguaçu, Paraná, Brazil.

Description:The Iguaçu Falls Trail, in the Iguaçu Falls National Park, is a 1,000-meter one-way trail, well-marked and easy to follow. It allows you to contemplate one of the seven wonders of nature, while walking through the forest. It is truly a breathtaking spectacle.

Catacumba Trail

Location:The Catacumba trail is located in the Catacumba Municipal Natural Park, in Lagoa Rodrigo de Freitas, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Description:The "Catacumba" trail is approximately 1,400 meters long, being easy and relatively quick, an ideal place for a short family outing.
